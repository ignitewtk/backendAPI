Steps for refreshing you the credentials:
1. Go to Google Console
2. At the left side menu, select and go to API&Service.
3. In OAuth 2.0 Client ID, click the name and enter item which you have configured the Gmail service.
4. Click "download Json" and rename as "credentials.json", and put in this directory (or the dir that you specified in code).
5. Start your service, and there will be a wepage prompted asking you for permission of accessing Gmail data.
